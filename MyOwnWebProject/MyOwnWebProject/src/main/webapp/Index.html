Types of Fitness


Endurance

Endurance activities, often referred to aerobic, increase your breathing and heart rates. These activities help keep you healthy, improve the health of your heart,lungs, and circulatory system.

Strength

Your muscular strength can make a big difference. Strong muscles help you stay independent and make everyday activities feel easier. Some people choose weights to help improve their strength.


Balance

Balance exercise help prevent fall,a common problem in older adults that can have serious consequences. Many lower-body strength exercises also improve your balance.

Flexibility

Stretching can improve your flexibility. Moving more freely will make it easier for you to reach down to tie your shoes or look over your shoulder.
